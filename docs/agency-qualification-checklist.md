- `Qualified for discovery` — useful learning fit; calibration not yet earned.
- `Qualified for calibration` — suitable, capable, and ready for the free deliverable.
- `Nurture` — plausible fit with a specific condition or later date.
- `Reject` — insufficient fit or no credible path to use and payment.
