# Validation plan

These are internal decision thresholds, not universal benchmarks.

- 75–100 targeted agency approaches
- 15–20 serious discovery conversations
- 10 qualified applicants
- 5 agencies willing to provide an ICP or target-account list
- 3 agencies willing to review manually prepared opportunities
- At least 2 paid pilots

Review conversion and interview evidence weekly. Separate polite interest from costly commitments: sharing an ICP, providing accounts, reviewing reports, and paying.
