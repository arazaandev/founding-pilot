# Manual pilot workflow

1. Add a potential agency to `templates/agency-outreach-tracker.csv` and record the next action.
2. Conduct a conversation using `discovery-call-guide.md`; capture exact language in `objection-and-language-log.md`.
3. Apply `agency-qualification-checklist.md` using manual judgment, not an automated score.
4. After three qualified calls, complete `three-call-synthesis.md` and review Gate A in `validation-gates.md`.
5. For an agency selected for free calibration, complete `calibration-onboarding-template.md`.
6. Prepare three manually verified opportunities after required inputs are complete.
7. The agency reviews each brief and records `Approve`, `Edit`, `Reject`, or `Postpone` in `templates/opportunity-feedback-record.csv`.
8. Record whether approved accounts were contacted and the research, review, revision, and variable costs.
9. Offer the paid founding pilot only when the evidence supports it; record the outcome and rationale.

Facts, sources, inferences, and hypotheses must remain visibly separate throughout. Evidence confidence remains separate from commercial opportunity scoring. A low-confidence opportunity cannot receive a `Contact now` recommendation without further verification.
