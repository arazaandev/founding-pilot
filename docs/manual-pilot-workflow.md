# Manual pilot workflow

1. Applicant submits the qualified application.
2. Team reviews score, breakdown, and behavioural answers.
3. Discovery conversation confirms the current workflow and pain.
4. Applicant shares an ICP and 5–20 target accounts.
5. Team prepares three manually verified sample opportunities.
6. Applicant reviews each sample and records approve, edit, reject, or postpone.
7. Team offers a paid concierge pilot when evidence supports it.
8. Applicant accepts or rejects; the team records the outcome and rationale.

Facts, sources, and hypotheses must remain visibly separate throughout.
